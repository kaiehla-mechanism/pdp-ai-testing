Engineering explainer

Why three stages change everything.

01
Three-stage telescoping columns Standard desks use two-stage legs — more gap, more lateral sway. Three stages distribute extension across more sections, each with <0.001″ precision glide tolerance. Near-zero wobble at any height.
02
T-frame, not C-frame C-frames anchor only at the back, leaving the front unsupported. Vernal's T-frame crossbar runs center-span — supporting load front-to-back and side-to-side at every height.
03
One motor per leg, perfectly synced Dual independent motors synchronize to <0.8mm height variance. If one encounters resistance, both stop and reverse. No tilt under uneven loads.
04
Third-party test report — publicly available Every spec is verified by an independent accredited lab, not self-reported. Load capacity, noise, lift cycles, column gap. We publish the full report. Most brands do not.
